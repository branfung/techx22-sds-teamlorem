A Little Bit About Us!

Mission

Populus designs is synonymous with uniqueness. We want to empower people to express themselves through their unique designs and personlaity so the whole world gets to meet who they are.

History

Populus desings was founded in 2022 by three software engineers with a passion for design. What started as a project for a class ended up being the most popular custom apparel website in the whole world!

Philosophy

We want to empower everyone in the world to show their true selfs because everyone is awesome and deserves to be seen. There is no one like you, not even your twin! So why hide yourself? :D
